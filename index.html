Kalari Therapeutic Massage Pre-booking Only

A natural and effective solution for body pain, stress relief, and overall rejuvenation. We provide Kalari massage, a traditional healing practice from Kerala that uses herbal oils and specialized pressure techniques to relieve deep-seated muscular tension, joint stiffness, and fatigue. It helps improve blood circulation, restore energy flow, and support both physical and mental well-being. Female guests will be attended by trained women therapists. Ayurvedic facial treatments are also available upon request, offering natural skin rejuvenation using herbal ingredients

Nearby Attractions

Payyambalam Beach

A serene beach ideal for long walks, sunsets, and family picnics.

St. Angelo Fort

A 16th-century fort with sea views, rich history, and colonial architecture.

Pazhassi Dam

A peaceful picnic spot surrounded by greenery and boating options.

Theyyam Ritual Art

Witness the ancient, dramatic dance rituals unique to North Kerala.

Palukachipara

perfect for a short scenic trek and dramatic sunrise or sunset views. Ideal for nature lovers and photographers

Mridanga Shaileshwari Temple

A powerful Devi temple located in Muzhakkunnu, known for spiritual calm and cultural richness.

Muzhappilangad drive-in Beach

Asia’s longest drive-in beach, Muzhappilangad offers a unique 4 km stretch of hard-packed sand where you can drive cars and bikes right along the shoreline.

Kottiyoor Temple

One of the oldest forest temples, famous for the 28-day Vaishakha Mahotsavam. It is associated with the Daksha Yaga legend and attracts thousands of devotees every year.

Mattnnur Sree Mahadeva Temple

Mattannur Mahadeva Temple is an ancient Shiva temple in Kannur, believed to be one of the 108 temples installed by Parashurama.

About Sreedevi Gardens

Sreedevi Gardens Home Stay is a peaceful, fully air-conditioned retreat nestled in the serene Maruthayi village near Mattanur. Surrounded by lush greenery and a traditional rural setting, it's located just 5 km from Kannur International Airport and 2.5 km from Mattanur bus stand.

We offer 6 thoughtfully maintained rooms — 2 spacious family rooms for up to 4 guests and 4 cozy double rooms for 2 guests. Each room features elegant furniture, a television, intercom, and bathrooms with bathtubs to ensure a comfortable and relaxing stay.

Guests can enjoy a complimentary breakfast and order meals . Additional services like Kalari massage and Ayurvedic facial treatments are available on prior request. We also provide stylish party halls and an inviting open café for casual gatherings.

Located amidst spiritual landmarks, Sreedevi Gardens is near renowned temples including Puthiya Bhagavathy, Muthappan, Sreekrishnan, and the historic Mridanga Saileswari and Kottiyoor Perumal temples.

Whether you seek spiritual calm, cultural richness, or a peaceful break in nature, Sreedevi Gardens offers the perfect blend of Kerala’s heritage and modern hospitality.
